About

I am a Software Engineering graduate student with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, particularly in developing intelligent systems that can interact with and support humans in meaningful ways . My research background is mainly in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Image Analysis . Through my academic and professional experiences, I have worked on designing AI-driven solutions while considering both technical performance and real-world usability. I am especially interested in trustworthy and human-centered AI, where models are not only accurate but also reliable, understandable, and beneficial for users. I am currently seeking opportunities to contribute to research groups working on Computer Vision, multimodal learning, and intelligent systems, with applications in areas such as Healthcare, Education, and Human-AI interaction.
